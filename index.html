About

What is Who's Behind That?

Who's Behind That? is an AI-powered tool that analyzes social media posts to detect narrative alignment — whose political agenda a post serves, and what context it leaves out. It uses Claude AI to score posts against a database of 56 geopolitical entities.

This tool is designed for researchers, journalists, and media-literate individuals who want to understand the information landscape around the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.

How it works

When you analyze a post, the text is sent to our server and scored by Claude AI against each entity's known interests, tactics, and public narrative. Results show which entities benefit most from the post being spread — both directly (primary alignment) and indirectly (secondary alignment).

Each scan uses one of your 10 daily credits. Credits reset at midnight.

Privacy

What we collect: The URL and text of posts you analyze, along with a randomly generated device identifier that links scans to your device. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information.

Who can see your scans: Your scan history is private to your device. The service operator may access anonymized scan data for the purpose of improving the tool.

Data retention: Scan data is retained for service operation purposes. You may request deletion by contacting us.
